# How-to-Use-the-App FAQ

Welcome to the patient portal FAQ! Here are instructions on how to navigate the application and update your profile details.

### How do I update my profile details?
To update your profile and basic details:
1. Log in to your account.
2. Click on your profile name or avatar in the top right corner of the navigation bar, and select "Profile Details" or go to the "Patients" tab and click on your card.
3. Locate the profile editing section.
4. Update fields like your weight, height, address, or phone number.
5. Click the "Save Changes" button to persist the updates in the database.

### How do I view my previous prediction history?
To view all risk predictions that have been run for you:
1. Navigate to the Patient Dashboard page.
2. Locate the "Prediction History" or "Recent Predictions" card.
3. Here you will see a list of past calculations, showing the date, risk scores, model used, and recommendations. You can also view a trend graph of your blood glucose levels and risk scores over time.

### How do I reset my password?
To change your current password:
1. Go to the Settings page or the User Profile screen.
2. Locate the "Security" or "Change Password" panel.
3. Enter your current password, type your new desired password, and confirm it in the confirmation input field.
4. Click "Update Password". Keep your password secure and do not share it with others.
