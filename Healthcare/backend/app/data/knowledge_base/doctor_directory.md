# CarePulse Doctor Directory and Referrals

The CarePulse portal features a team of specialists to support your long-term health and wellness. Based on your risk prediction scores and lab values, you may receive recommendation referrals to consult specific specialists.

### Specialized Departments
1. **Endocrinology (Diabetes and Metabolic Care)**
   * **Specialist**: Dr. Gregory House
   * **When to consult**: Triggered if your risk score is elevated (>80%) or your fasting glucose is high (>140 mg/dL). Focuses on advanced insulin management and diabetic treatment.
2. **Cardiology (Heart and Blood Pressure)**
   * **Specialist**: Dr. John Watson
   * **When to consult**: Recommended if your diastolic blood pressure is elevated (>90 mm Hg). Focuses on cardiovascular monitoring and blood pressure regulation.
3. **Clinical Nutrition (Weight and Lifestyle)**
   * **Specialist**: Dr. Sarah Smith
   * **When to consult**: Recommended if your Body Mass Index (BMI) is in the obese range (>=30.0). Focuses on customized diets, meal planning, and metabolic weight management.
4. **General Medicine (Primary Care)**
   * **Specialist**: Dr. Jane Grey
   * **When to consult**: Standard annual wellness checkups, routine diagnostics review, and general health screenings.

### How to Book an Appointment
From the Patient Dashboard, locate the "Upcoming Appointments" section, click "+ Book New", select the desired specialist from the drop-down, pick a scheduled time, add notes on your symptoms, and confirm.
