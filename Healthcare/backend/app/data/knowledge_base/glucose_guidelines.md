# American Diabetes Association (ADA) Fasting Glucose Guidelines

Fasting blood glucose levels: A level of 99 mg/dL or lower is normal. A level between 100 and 125 mg/dL indicates prediabetes (impaired fasting glucose, reflecting insulin resistance). A fasting glucose level of 126 mg/dL or higher on two separate tests indicates diabetes. During an Oral Glucose Tolerance Test (OGTT), 140 to 199 mg/dL indicates prediabetes, while 200 mg/dL or higher indicates diabetes.
