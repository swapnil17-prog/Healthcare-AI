# AHA/ACC Hypertension Guidelines

Blood pressure stages: Normal blood pressure is less than 120 mm Hg systolic and less than 80 mm Hg diastolic. Elevated blood pressure is 120-129 systolic and less than 80 diastolic. Stage 1 Hypertension is defined as 130-139 systolic or 80-89 diastolic. Stage 2 Hypertension is defined as 140 or higher systolic or 90 or higher diastolic. A hypertensive crisis is higher than 180 systolic and/or higher than 120 diastolic, requiring immediate emergency medical attention.
